About Celiac Disease Meal Planner

Celiac Disease Meal Planner is a web application that helps people with celiac disease to plan their meals. The application provides a list of recipes that are safe for people with celiac disease to eat. The application also allows users to search for recipes based on their dietary restrictions and preferences. Users can also create meal plans and save their favorite recipes.
